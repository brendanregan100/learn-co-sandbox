BCMC BET brings you "The Open"

Entry deadline 18.00 GMT 18 July

House Rules: Choose 7 golfers per rules below

  1. Pick 2 from top 12 golfers
  2. Pick 1 American
  3. Pick 1 European
  4. Pick 1 RoW (Rest of World)
  5. Pick 2 wildcards (must have combined ranking of 60 or above outside top 12)
  6. Tie Breaks: (A) Pick winners score to par and (B) worst inidividual of the top 12 golfers

Winner:
  • The winner will be decided by the 4/7 best scores from their players
    • A miniumu of 4 of your golfers need to make the cut or you will be eliminated on Friday evening. If there is a tie, it will first be decided by tiebreak (A) and then if still a tie, by tiebreak (B)
